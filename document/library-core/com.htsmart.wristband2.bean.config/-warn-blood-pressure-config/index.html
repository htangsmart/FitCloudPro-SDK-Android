If isExtWarnBloodPressure is true, it represents wristband support the blood pressure warning function.

This setting will affect whether blood pressure warning is enabled and set the warning value. For systolic and diastolic blood pressure, you can set their upper and lower limits respectively.

When blood pressure exceeds the preset range and lasts for a certain period of time, the wristband will vibrate for warning.
